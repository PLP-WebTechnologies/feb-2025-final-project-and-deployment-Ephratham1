About Us

Our Story

Kasi-Empire Butchery was founded in 2010 by the Nkosi family with a simple mission: to provide the community with the highest quality meat products at fair prices.

What started as a small local butchery has grown into a beloved institution in our community, known for exceptional quality and outstanding service.

We take pride in sourcing our meat from ethical local farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainable practices.

Our Values

Quality

We are committed to providing only the highest quality meat products to our customers.

Community

We believe in supporting our local community and creating meaningful relationships.

Sustainability

We work with farmers who use sustainable and ethical farming practices.

Service

We are dedicated to providing excellent customer service and building trust.
